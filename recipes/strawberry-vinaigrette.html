Easy Strawberry Vinaigrette

This strawberry salad dressing changed the way I eat salads.

It's an easy, delicious, and low-calorie vinaigrette. I like it with a simple spinach, almond, and feta salad.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces frozen strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Blend strawberries, honey, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, salt, and black pepper together in a blender until smooth.

    2. Done!
